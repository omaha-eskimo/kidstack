# School Meeting Plan

## Meeting Goal

Choose two low-risk support experiments and define how the group will evaluate them after two weeks.

## Proposed Agenda

### 1. Shared ground truth — 5 minutes

Review [[school-meeting-ground-truth]] and correct anything unsupported.

### 2. Fill evidence gaps — 10 minutes

Use [[school-meeting-gap-analysis]] to collect missing classroom and lunch observations.

### 3. Choose experiments — 10 minutes

Proposed options:

- demonstrate mastery before repetitive practice
- preview one difficult daily transition visually
- offer a discreet quiet reset after lunch

### 4. Define measurement — 5 minutes

- Who records observations?
- What specific change are we looking for?
- When will the group review results?

## Desired Outputs

- Two agreed experiments
- Named owner for each experiment
- One shared observation format
- Review meeting scheduled within two weeks
