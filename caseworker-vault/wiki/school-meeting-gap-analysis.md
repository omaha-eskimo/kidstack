# School Meeting Gap Analysis

## Summary

The existing sources suggest useful patterns but do not yet support confident conclusions. The next meeting should fill specific evidence gaps.

## Gaps to Fill From School

- Two concrete examples of work Maya found engaging
- Two examples of disengagement, including what happened immediately before it
- Observations from lunch and major transitions
- Evidence of which skills have already been mastered

## Gaps to Fill From Home

- A short record of after-school energy and recovery
- Maya's own descriptions, kept separate from adult interpretation
- Examples of activities that sustain attention without prompting

## Questions to Put on the Table

1. What pattern do all adults actually agree they have observed?
2. What are we currently inferring without enough evidence?
3. Which two changes can we test without waiting for a perfect explanation?
4. What will we measure, and when will we review it?
