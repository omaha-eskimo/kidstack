# Home Environment Plan

## Current Experiments

- Use a visible weekend plan and mark uncertain items as flexible.
- Offer twenty quiet minutes after school before asking detailed questions.
- Capture observations without interpreting them in the moment.
- Invite Maya to rate strategies as helpful, neutral, or unhelpful.

## What Success Might Look Like

- Maya communicates needs with less pressure.
- Plan changes require less recovery time.
- Parent can distinguish recurring patterns from memorable one-time events.
