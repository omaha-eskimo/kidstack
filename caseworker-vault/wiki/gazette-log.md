# Gazette Log

Running record of what's landing and what isn't in the daily newspaper.
Add short notes here after any day worth noting. Claude Code reads this
during curation passes to weight the content bank.

## How to add an entry

Just drop a line with a date and a note. No format required.

> 2026-07-16 — Archimedes quote didn't land, Maya barely looked at it.  
> 2026-07-17 — The "cook dinner" mission was a hit, she did it without prompting.  
> 2026-07-18 — She's been asking about bridges lately. Add more engineering content.

During a curation pass, Claude Code will read these notes and recommend
additions, removals, and reweighting in the content bank.
