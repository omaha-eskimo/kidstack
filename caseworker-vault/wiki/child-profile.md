# Child Profile

## Summary

Maya Chen is a fictional nine-year-old learner who is highly curious, visually creative, and motivated by meaningful challenges. She can become overwhelmed during noisy transitions and disengage when work feels repetitive.

## Current Understanding

- Age: 9
- School year: Grade 4
- Strong interests: architecture, maps, animals, drawing, and building miniature environments
- Learning pattern: engages deeply with open-ended problems and loses momentum during repeated practice
- Communication pattern: often explains difficult feelings more clearly after time to decompress
- Regulation pattern: drawing, advance notice, and quiet space appear helpful

## Durable Themes

- Challenge and autonomy increase engagement.
- Busy transitions may cost more energy than adults can see.
- Quiet withdrawal should not automatically be interpreted as lack of interest.
- Maya responds well when adults explain the purpose behind a task.
